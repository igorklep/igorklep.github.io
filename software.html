Igor Klep - Software

NCSOStools

NCSOStools is an open source MATLAB toolbox for

  • symbolic computation with polynomials in noncommuting variables;

  • constructing and solving sum of hermitian squares (with commutators) programs for polynomials in noncommuting variables.

Written together with and , it has a dedicated website https://ncsostools.github.io/.

ChemComp

Chemical Reaction Network Software (under Mathematica). Features:

  • Chemical Reaction Network Jacobian

  • Deficiency and YAk Representation of Chemical Network

Written with , Karl Fredrickson and Vitaly Katsnelson. Its dedicated website is http://www.math.ucsd.edu/~chemcomp/.
